Lu Ling

I am Lu Ling, a PhD student in the IDEADs Lab and CGV Lab at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Aniket Bera.

My research interests focus on artificial intelligence in computer vision, including 3D content generation and reconstruction using learning-based 3D deep models. We collected the DL3DV-10K , a real-world scene dataset to forge a path toward a foundation model for learning 3D representation.

I am a NVIDIA Research intern in 2024 summer, and working with Zhaoshuo Li, Chen-Hsuan Lin, Tsung-Yin Lin, and Ming-Yu Liu
