Lu Ling

I am Lu Ling, a PhD candidate in the IDEADs Lab at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Aniket Bera.

I am passionate about spatial intelligence and physical AI. I build scalable generative models for controllable 3D scene generation / world models, with an emphasis on data scaling through large-scale real-world capture and synthetic scene composition. The goal is to enable virtual content creation and editing, and to provide spatially grounded world understanding for embodied AI. I led the DL3DV-10K project, a large-scale real-world scene dataset to forge a path toward a foundation model for learning 3D representation.

I am a member of technical staff at World Labs working on spatial intelligence in fall 2025, and an NVIDIA Research intern at NVIDIA Deep Imagination Research in summer and fall 2024.


I am looking for full-time Research Scientist and Member of Technical Staff positions in industry. Feel free to reach out to me via email!
